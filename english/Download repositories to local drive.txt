How to download OOHG's repositories to local disk:

Download "Git" from https://git-scm.com/download
Install
Download "GitHub Desktop" desfromde https://desktop.github.com/
Install
Open GitHub Desktop.
Click "Clone a repository"
Click "URL"
At "Repository ..." enter https://github.com/oohg/core.git
At "Local path" enter C:\OOHG (the folder must not exist).
Click "Clone"

How to download the other repositories (distros, doc, ejemplos, fmt, ide, samples):

Click "File" menu
Click option "Clone a repository"
Click "URL"
At "Repository ..." enter the corresponding address
   https://github.com/oohg/distros.git
   https://github.com/oohg/doc.git
   https://github.com/oohg/ejemplos.git
   https://github.com/oohg/fmt.git
   https://github.com/oohg/ide.git
   https://github.com/oohg/samples.git
At "Local path" enter
   C:\OOHG\distros
   C:\OOHG\doc
   C:\OOHG\ejemplos
   C:\OOHG\fmt
   C:\OOHG\ide
   C:\OOHG\samples
Click "Clone"
